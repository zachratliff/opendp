Google Differential Privacy¶

Google Differential Privacy consists of libraries to generate ε- and (ε, δ)-differentially private statistics over datasets.

Additional Projects¶

For additional related projects see “Available Tools for Differentially Private Analysis” in Appendix C of the Handbook on Using Administrative Data for Research and Evidence-based Policy.
