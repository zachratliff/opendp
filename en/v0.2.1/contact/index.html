Contact¶

A great way to get in contact with the OpenDP community is GitHub Discussions.

To subscribe to the mailing list, please use the sign up form.

You can reach OpenDP on Twitter at @opendp_org.

To report bugs, please use the issue tracker.

To report security issues, please email security@opendp.org.

For general inquiries, please email info@opendp.org.
